Peruvian Split Peas

Description

Peruvian eat legums in many ways, especially as a side dish. Serve with rice, grilled fish and a salad and you will have a balanced meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of split peas
  • 1 White onion
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 2 Plum tomatoes
  • Salt and Ground Black pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cooked white rice
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Wash the split peas
  2. Dice the white onion, and prepare the garlic
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pot and fry the onion with garlic until brown
  4. Roughly chop the plum tomatoes and add them into the pot
  5. Continue cooking until the onions start turning brown
  6. Add the split peas to the pot
  7. Add water until the split peas are covered. Add salt (to taste) and ground black pepper (to taste). Sit the mix and bring it to a boil over medium-high heat.
  8. When water starts boiling, lower the heat to the low and cook until the split peas are soft. Stir every 5 minutes
  9. When the peas are soft and everything has cooked down, it is ready to serve.
  10. Fry the egg
  11. Serve with rice and the fried egg on top, and enjoy!
